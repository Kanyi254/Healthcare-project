The dataset consists of 55,500 entries with 15 columns. Here's an overview of the columns:

Name: Patient's name.
Age: Patient's age.
Gender: Patient's gender.
Blood Type: Patient's blood type.
Medical Condition: The medical condition diagnosed.
Date of Admission: Date when the patient was admitted.
Doctor: The attending doctor.
Hospital: The hospital where the patient was treated.
Insurance Provider: The patient's insurance provider.
Billing Amount: The amount billed for the patient's treatment.
Room Number: The room number where the patient was accommodated.
Admission Type: Type of admission (e.g., urgent, emergency, elective).
Discharge Date: Date when the patient was discharged.
Medication: Medication prescribed.
Test Results: Results of any tests conducted (e.g., normal, abnormal).
